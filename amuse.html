The Onion

The Onion is a parody news site that publishes articles parodying current news topics. The headlines of The Onion articles are characterized by straightforward humor. The reason I find The Onion funny is because the headlines are always delivered very seriously.

The Onion

Cookie Clicker

Cookie Clicker is a web game where the player clicks to make cookies (at the start, only chocolate chip) that can be used to buy upgrades that will auto-click for the player. The goal of the game is to amass lots of cookies, but the game is theoretically endless. I usually get bored at around a trillion cookies (which takes a few days of idle auto-clicking) so I don't know if the game can actually end.

Cookie Clicker

Disney's Fastpass

Defunctland is a YouTube channel that publishes video essays based on amusement parks. Their video essay on the history of Disney's Fastpass system is pretty cool, kind of long, and funny at times.
