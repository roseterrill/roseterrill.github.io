Overview

Cookie Clicker is a web game where the player clicks to make cookies. The goal of the game is to amass lots of cookies, but the game is theoretically endless. I usually get bored at around a trillion cookies (which takes a few days of idle auto-clicking) so I don't know if the game can actually end.

How to Play

  1. Click on the chocolate chip cookie in the center. You can't miss it.
  2. Keep clicking until you amass 100 cookies.
  3. Click on the cookie in the bottom left corner labeled +1 cpc. You are now baking 2 cookies per 1 click.
  4. Click on the cookie in the bottom left corner labeled +1 cps. You are now baking 1 cookie per 1 second, which means your cookies will amass automatically.
  5. Continue clicking or leave your browser running to amass more cookies. Once you have more cookies, buy more upgrades to increase your cpc or cps.
  6. Your goal is to make as many cookies as possible. There is no end. Good luck!
