Cookie Clicker

Overview

Cookie Clicker is a web game where the player clicks to make cookies. The goal of the game is to amass lots of cookies, but the game is theoretically endless. I usually get bored at around a trillion cookies (which takes a few days of idle auto-clicking) so I don't know if the game can actually end.

How to Play

  • Click on the chocolate chip cookie in the center. You can't miss it.
  • Keep clicking until you amass 100 cookies.
  • Click on the cookie in the bottom left corner labeled +1 cpc. You are now baking 2 cookies per 1 click.
  • Click on the cookie in the bottom left corner labeled +1 cps. You are now baking 1 cookie per 1 second, which means your cookies will amass automatically.
  • Continue clicking or leave your browser running to amass more cookies. Once you have more cookies, buy more upgrades to increase your cpc or cps.
  • Your goal is to make as many cookies as possible. There is no end. Good luck!
